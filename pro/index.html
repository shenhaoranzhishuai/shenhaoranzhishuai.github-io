Reduce your carbon footprint

A carbon footprint is the amount of carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions associated with all activities of a person or other entity (such as a building, company, country, etc.). It includes direct emissions, such as those from the burning of fossil fuels in manufacturing, heating and transport, as well as those required to generate electricity related to the goods and services consumed.Take a look at the picture to the right to see what carbon emissions one person may cause!
